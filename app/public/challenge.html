He takes his SA house music seriously, maybe a bit too much

This music will make you move. It is an eclectic mix of instruments from salsa, samba and afrobeats. “It's been a treat to expose our music and our culture to the world on this level,” he said backstage at New York’s Full Moon Festival last month.

“The African rhythm, even when it's not obvious, is in our music. I think our responsibility now is to make sure that it goes to the mainstage, and it's not pigeonholed to ‘world music’ or put on smaller stages as ‘world music artists.’ We can be where everyone is.”
